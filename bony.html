Surgery For Bony Tumors

Spinal tumor surgery is done to treat tumors developed anywhere on the spinal column from tissues, vertebrae to the spinal cord. These tumors could have either affected the spine primarily or they can be metastatic, meaning the tumor has spread from any other part of the body to the spine.

A spinal tumor is the abnormal growth of tissues in the spinal column. Depending upon the location of the tumor, pain can be felt in the spine or the other parts of the body. If any spinal nerve becomes damaged or compressed, its neurological effect can be observed from weakness or numbness in the limbs and can lead to paralysis in extreme cases.
