about us

our clinic is made for you to be smiling all the time

Welcome to Neuro Center, where we prioritize your neurological health and well-being. Our clinic, led by Dr. Gangesh Gunjan, has been providing exceptional healthcare services in Delhi since 2010. We specialize in treating a wide range of neurological conditions, ensuring that our patients receive the highest quality of care.

At Neuro Center, our mission is to offer comprehensive neurological care with a personal touch. Our experienced team of professionals is dedicated to diagnosing and treating neurological disorders with precision and compassion. Whether it's brain, spine, or pain management, we are here to help you lead a healthier life.
